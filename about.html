Russ Cooper

Russ Cooper is a visionary entrepreneur, investor, and outspoken advocate for workplace safety and economic justice. His journey began not in the boardrooms of Silicon Valley, but in the very trenches of the modern workforce, where he experienced firsthand the challenges faced by everyday employees. A workplace injury early in his career gave Russ an unshakable belief in the right to fair compensation and the power of innovative disruption. A hard worker with a keen eye for identifying inefficiencies in outdated systems, he attacked his employer through the legal system. His record-breaking workplace lawsuit has championed workers rights and made him the first billionaire through a class-action lawsuit.


A devoted family man and hard worker, Russ credits his resilience and vision to his upbringing, where he was taught the value of perseverance and standing up for what is right. Today, he channels that same energy into investing in companies that challenge the status quo. Whether through cutting-edge advancements in AI automation or his relentless push for transparency in data security, Russ continues to redefine what it means to be a leader in the digital age—proving that true success isn’t measured by where you start, but by the impact you make.


This site is maintained by his eldest son, Todd.

The Ironic Commencement
Behold My Legendary Folly
Satire Manifesto – a chronicle of my absurd rise and the farce of tech pretension.
I’ve watched the so‐called pioneers prattle on about “innovation” while I quietly laughed at the absurdity. Back in the day, I stood before a crowd of wannabe geniuses—smirking at every naïve claim.
Why should anyone take themselves seriously when the joke is on all of us? I’ve never bowed to convention; instead, I revel in my own irreverence. Every remark I make is a jab at the pretension festering in high-tech lore.
Now, as the world clamors for a memoir from the mastermind who defied the ordinary, I offer you my tale—a riotous account of deliberate folly, mockery, and a healthy dose of self-deprecation. Welcome to the legend of Sam Founder.
